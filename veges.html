Exploring Geothermal

Direct Use in

Eburu, Kenya.

A little detail about Eburu Project.

Geothermal energy is a valuable sustainable resource in Kenya, and leveraging its potential in diverse sectors can drive economic growth and promote environmental sustainability. This challenge aims to explore the direct use of geothermal energy in multiple value chains, including geotourism, poultry farming, dairy farming, health, drying technology, pre-cooking food, and fish farming. By integrating geothermal energy into these sectors, we can create better business cases, improve productivity, and contribute to the country's development.

Objectives of Triple Helix in Eburu.

Triple Helix is purposing to act in the overall interests of the community with respect to:

Establishing a collaborative platform with stakeholders.

Engage students from the partnering academic institutions.

Develop and implement educational programs to train students.

More of our objectives.

Facilitate the establishment of student-led businesses.

Conduct research and development activities in collaboration with industrial academia partners.

Collaborate with local communities to assess their energy needs and develop sustainable solutions.

Promote community engagement and knowledge transfer by organizing workshops and seminars to empower residents.
